Who We Are

Welcome to Charming Castle, where we take balloon artistry to a whole new level! Located in the heart of Toronto, we specialize in creating jaw-dropping balloon decorations that will leave your guests gasping in awe.

From whimsical balloon arches that frame your special moments to elegant bouquets that add a pop of color, we’ve got it all. Whether it’s a wedding, a birthday bash, or a bar mitzvah, our balloons are the life of the party!

And let’s not forget our rental services! Need hardware for your balloon dreams? We offer everything from balloon stands to decor tables, ensuring your event is not just decorated, but absolutely unforgettable!
